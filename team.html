Kakooza Hakim

Founder & Squad Captain

Hakim Kakooza is a change-maker with a mission to democratize healthcare services in low-income communities using technology and community power. Expected to graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Entrepreneurial Leadership, majoring in Healthcare Policy and Advocacy from African Leadership University in 2025, Hakim has worked as a Program IT Intern, Alumni Relations Coordinator, and Assistant Trainer at Fundi Robotics Ltd.
